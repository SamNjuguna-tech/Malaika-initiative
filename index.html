MALAIKA INITIATIVE FOR PEOPLE WITH DISABILITY (M.I.P.D)

Our mission is to empower, build capacity and improve the health, spiritual, and social-economic status of children living With disability and their caregivers.

Our objective is to identify, support, manage and create a knowledgeable community that is able to make informed decisions for persons living with physical and intelectual disabilities.
