Our Services

Health-Care

At Malaika Initiative For People With Disability we provide Physio and Occupational Therapy to the children.
In addition Psycho-social Support to parents/guardians and families.

Spiritual Nourishment

We work closely with the church for spiritual nourishment through bible study, prayers and spiritual cancelling to parents, families and people with disability.

Feeding

A balanced diet is crucial for a child's growth and development. Malaika Initiative for People with Disability, provides healthy meals to the children (according to their needs) at the day-care center. Individuals and/or groups come in handy, donating variuous food supplies

Community Outreach

In order to reduce stigma, Malaika Initiative for People With Disability reaches out to families and communites to educate on matters of physical and mental disability such as:

  • Causes and prevention.
  • Early detection and intervention.
  • Challenges of bringing up children with mental and or physical disability.
  • Develompment of positive mental attitude as regards to children and/or families with mental/physical disability.
  • Linkage with instituitions that provide formal education to children with special needs.
