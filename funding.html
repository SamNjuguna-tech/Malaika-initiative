BE AN ANGEL FOR A CHILD TODAY



Every child deserves proper health care, nutrition, education, etc. Children with disability, requires much more care which costs more to bring them up. Things even get tougher when the child comes from a poor family. Malaika Initiative For People With Disability caters for such children. They come from very poor and needy families- 50% being cared for by single mothers who have been abandoned by their husbands. Some of the parents are indeed so poor such that, they struggle to provide food to their families. By donating, you provide much needed support to meet the very basic needs of a deserving child. Every penny matters, and goes towards that.
